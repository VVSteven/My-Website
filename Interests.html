Listening to Music

Listening to music is one of my greatest passions. I find excitement, inspiration, and joy in listening to cool sounds. Songs that have a rhythm that makes me want to dance along and bump my head violently.

I love energetic, punky, rap albums and crazy tempoed, fast-paced dance music. I can enjoy listening to different genres, but only when I have a feel to listen to them; otherwise, I enjoy hearing the same thing.
